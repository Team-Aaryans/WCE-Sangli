Who are we?

We are Team Aaryans An official All Terrain Vehicle Racing Team from Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli Established in 2011 We strongly encourage you to join Team Aaryans Club today!

What do we do?

We build an ATV which is tested on different challenging terrains. The vehicle is also tested based on cost, quality of fabrication and reliability


What is Baja?

Baja is an Automotive Design, manufacturing and racing event organised by SAEINDIA and sponsored by Mahindra from 2007 till date .The objective of Baja is to design, build and race off-road vehicles that can withstand the harshest elements of rough terrain by Engineering Students across the globe
