KOSHAL GRAPE 250 H

KOSHAL GRAPE 250 H is a tractor mounted sprayer for vineyards. It is Compact, Easy to Attach/ Detach and sturdy in design. This can be operated by 18 HP and above tractors. It uses Reverse Axial Blower System which has great air suction capability and reverse design helps to restrict the entry of leaves, grass or other particles into the blower. To get the uniform air output on both sides of blower, a fiber cone and Baffles system is designed. This helps to improve the Grape production FEATURES Operated by 18 HP Tractor and Above Complete Canopy coverage in spraying and dipping 600 Ltr. Tank. Also Available in Italian imported tank. 6-16 Tyre from Birla / JK/ CEAT 50 LPM HTP from A1- Shakti/Lu-shyoung Ceramic Piston American Tee-jet Nozzles -08 Nos. 24” Dragon Fan with Reverse Casing Design Also available in Italian Udor company Diaphragm Pump
